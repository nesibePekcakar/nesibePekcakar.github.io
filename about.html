About Nesibe

I’ve always been drawn to software and artificial intelligence. Through the science-fiction books and films I grew up with, these fields sparked my curiosity long before I studied engineering.

During my studies, I earned a full scholarship, Erasmus funding, and a merit award given to only three students in the department each year—reflecting my discipline and dedication to learning.

I focus on building systems that are practical, user-centered, and technically solid. Quality, clarity, and thoughtful execution guide every project I build.

I love engineering, solving problems, learning, and creating technology that genuinely works.

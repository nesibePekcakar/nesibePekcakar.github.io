About Nesibe

I’ve always been drawn to intelligent systems. Long before studying engineering, I was immersed in science-fiction books filled with futuristic technologies and AI—ideas that sparked a genuine excitement for the world of software.

I graduated with several academic achievements: a full scholarship, Erasmus funding, and a merit award given to only a few students each year. These accomplishments reflect years of discipline, curiosity, and a deep love for learning.

I approach projects from both a technical and user-centered perspective— understanding system needs, researching tools, building structures carefully, and improving where necessary. Quality and efficiency guide my work.

My strengths appear in action: how I think, how I solve problems, and how deeply I commit to the work. I aim to specialize in developing software enriched with AI—tools that make life easier, smarter, and more efficient.

If there is one message I want visitors to take away, it’s this:
I love solving problems, learning fast, and building technology that truly works.
